Marsupialmancer's Birthright rules tutorial

Specs

MM battles have units use 2 values:

  • The Battlefield Challenge Rating or BCR, lsited under the shield icon and blue score
  • A contextual bonus to the roll listed under the sword icon and dark red score

Each unit has 2 hp, no other score is used.

Okay, let's get going

  1. Select terrain, weather (if not rolled) and visibility) (limited if night or heavy fog
  2. Now start adding units, if you want them to be identified easily select the "battle->use ordinals" menu.

Done! you may start your battle. Each turn all units will roll to survive. Some units may gain 1 BCR and be promoted to veterans.
